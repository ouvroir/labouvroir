# Display: a web app to document collection hangings

The Ouvroir d'histoire de l'art et de museologie numériques is working on the development of an application to document the hanging of collections. This application is intended to support all research operations, from the collection of historical information, to the formulation of hypotheses and the recording of results. 

Discussion on the ins and outs of the project with Marie Fraser, Emmanuel Château-Dutier, Zoë Renaudie and David Valentine, November 5, 2025. 

The session will take place in hybrid mode at the University of Montreal, Lionel-Groulx Pavilion, room C-8132 and on [Zoom](https://umontreal.zoom.us/j/82480661654?pwd=cUlzb09hZ3lkd2UvcmpPbTdmQkZBQT09).
